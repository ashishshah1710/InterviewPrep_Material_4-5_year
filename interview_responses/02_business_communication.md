**Tips:** Remind, be polite, mention the previous attempt.

### 1.3 Escalation Emails

**Tips:** Be factual, neutral, state why escalation is necessary.

**Example:**
```

I am escalating the request for production database access, initially submitted to Alex (details attached). The unresolved access has delayed investigation of issue #453 for over a week.

Could you please assist in expediting the approval?

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
Priya
```
