## 9. How to Negotiate Salary in English

1. **Be Polite & Professional.**
2. **Express Enthusiasm.**
3. **State Expectations (preferably backed by market data).**
4. **Ask for Time to Consider, if Necessary.**
5. **Counter-Offer, if Appropriate.**

### Example Negotiation Dialogue

- **Company:** "Our offer is $120,000 with standard benefits. Does that sound acceptable?"
- **You:**  
  > "Thank you very much for the offer. I'm very excited about the role and the team. Based on my research and experience, I was expecting a base salary closer to $135,000. Is there flexibility to adjust the offer?"

- **Alternative if not ready to respond:**  
  > "Thank you for the offer! Could I have some time to review the details and get back to you in the next two days?"

- **If salary can't change:**  
  > "Thank you for considering my request. If the base salary is fixed, is there any flexibility in signing bonus, professional development budget, or extra vacation days?"
